We organize our work around four overlapping themes.

## 🔬 Drug Safety Surveillance
Active and passive surveillance of adverse drug reactions using KAERS, FAERS, and Korean claims databases.
Recent focus: immune checkpoint inhibitor (ICI) related toxicities — hepatotoxicity, cachexia, nephrotoxicity.

## 📊 Real-World Evidence (RWE)
Comparative effectiveness and safety studies using nationwide health insurance data (HIRA, NHIS) and hospital EHRs.
Focus areas: oncology drugs, antibiotics, antidepressants, dopamine agonists.

## 🤖 Prediction Modeling
Development and validation of clinical risk prediction models, including machine-learning approaches for AKI, AMS, and blood pressure.

## 🏗 Common Data Model (CDM) Research
Methodology and applied research using OMOP-CDM, including pilot studies and cohort construction for CDI, sepsis, and other infectious diseases.
