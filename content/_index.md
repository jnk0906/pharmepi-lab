We conduct **pharmacoepidemiologic research** generating real-world evidence on drug safety and effectiveness.
Our work uses Korean national claims data, the Common Data Model (CDM), and adverse event reporting databases (KAERS, FAERS)
to inform clinical and regulatory decision-making.

**Research areas:** Drug safety surveillance · Real-world evidence · Prediction modeling · Pharmacovigilance · CDM-based studies
