We welcome motivated students and collaborators interested in pharmacoepidemiology and real-world evidence.

## 🎓 Prospective Graduate Students

We are currently accepting applications for both **Master's** and **Doctoral** programs in the College of Pharmacy, Pusan National University.

**Ideal candidates have:**
- Background in pharmacy, medicine, statistics, epidemiology, or related quantitative fields
- Interest in healthcare data analytics and drug safety
- Experience or interest in SAS, R, Python, or SQL is a plus (not required)

**To apply:**
1. Review our [research areas](/research/) to identify alignment with your interests
2. Email Prof. Jeon (`njeon@pusan.ac.kr`) with your CV, a brief statement of interest, and undergraduate transcript
3. Information about the formal admission process is available at the [PNU Graduate School](https://gs.pusan.ac.kr/)

## 🔬 Undergraduate Research Students

Undergraduate pharmacy students at PNU interested in research experience are encouraged to reach out.
We typically host 2–4 undergraduate research students per semester.

## 🤝 Collaborators

For research collaborations, data-sharing agreements, or consulting on pharmacoepidemiologic methods,
please contact Prof. Jeon directly.
